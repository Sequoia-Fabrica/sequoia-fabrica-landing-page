We are Sequoia Fabrica, an intergenerational community workshop in San Francisco’s Potrero Hill neighborhood fostering the next generation of makers, designers and craftspeople. We’re home to a wood and textile workshop, 3D printers and a laser cutter, electronics, crafts and fine arts stations.

We are a volunteer-run 501(c)3 non-profit, and host classes, events and workshop hours open to the public.

We offer memberships for makers who want to access the space and equipment outside of classes. Want to teach a class or organize an event at Sequoia? Please contact us.
