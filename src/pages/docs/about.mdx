# About Sequoia Fabrica

Welcome to Sequoia Fabrica, a 501(c)(3) non-profit, community workshop and makerspace in San Francisco.

Our organization was founded November 2023, and is 100% volunteer run. We are supported by donations and memberships. Most of our equipment is second-hand or donated.

## What We Offer 

* Weekly [classes and events](./events), open to the public, on subjects including arts, crafts, technology, and tools. 
* Affordable [memberships](./membership), equipment and infrastructure for learning, arts, and creative projects.
* Physical space for exhibitions, meetups, community and educational events.
## Why We Do What We Do

To build great things, you need good [tools](https://wiki.sequoiafabrica.org/wiki/Main_Page) and good community. Sequoia Fabrica offers both! In the tradition of [makerspaces](https://www.makerspaces.com/what-is-a-makerspace/) and [hackerspaces](https://hackerspaces.org), we offer shared access to high-quality tools and machinery in an environment where learning, sharing, and building together is the norm. 

We believe in the transformative power of access to tools and technology. Our mission is to democratize the ability to create, innovate, and explore artistic and scientific realms including but not limited to: Fine Arts, Electronics, Sewing and Woodcrafts. 

## Who We Are

As of July 2024, our community is around twenty members and volunteers. Together we teach classes, take care of administrative tasks, maintain our machines and facility. We have a range of interests including woodworking, fiber arts, electronics, digital fabrication, and fine arts. Our members include designers, educators, engineers, public servants, service workers, and working artists. 

## Join Our Community

Whether you're a seasoned maker, a curious beginner, or someone who believes in the power of community and creativity, there's a place for you here. We're always searching for [volunteers](https://wiki.sequoiafabrica.org/wiki/Volunteers) to help maintain the space, run classes, and be welcoming community members.
