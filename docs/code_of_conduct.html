Code of Conduct

This code of conduct applies to all events and spaces sponsored by Sequoia Fabrica, including our physical locations, website, mailing lists, social media, and chat.

Some Sequoia Fabrica-sponsored spaces may have additional rules that participants must follow. Participants are responsible for knowing and abiding by these rules.

Harassment

The Sequoia Fabrica (SF) community is dedicated to providing a harassment-free experience for everyone. We do not tolerate harassment of participants in any form, including:

  • Offensive comments related to gender, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, appearance, race, ethnicity, religion, etc.
  • Intimidation, stalking, doxxing
  • Disrupting events
  • Threats of violence
  • Encouraging self-harm
  • Continued communication after requests to stop
  • Physical contact without consent
  • Unwelcome sexual attention, unsolicited sexual images or content
  • Deliberate misgendering and dead-naming
  • Deliberately revealing private information without consent, except as necessary to protect other community members from intentional abuse
  • Harassing photography or recording, including logging online activity for harassment purposes
  • Spamming, denial of service attacks, hacking personal devices or accounts without consent

The Sequoia Fabrica will not act on:

  • Reasonable communication of personal boundaries
  • Attempts to troll or undermine the code of conduct process

Reporting

If you experience or notice harassment, or have other concerns, reach out to sequoiafabrica@gmail.com.

Workshop Policies

  • Follow all safety procedures, as instructed by maintainers
  • Report unsafe conditions, broken equipment, and other incidents promptly
  • Keep the workspace clean and orderly
  • Use tools and materials responsibly, as directed by maintainers

Consequences

Members are empowered to warn or expel anyone violating this code of conduct. Further sanctions may be applied at the discretion of the Board.

The Board may take any action they deem appropriate, including warning an offender, suspension of membership, temporary or permanent expulsion from our spaces and events without refund or notice.

Contact the Board with any questions or concerns in person or at sequoiafabrica@gmail.com.
